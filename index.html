About Easy Meal 🍽 Easy Meal is a simple food ordering system created to solve a common problem in hostels.In many hostels, one person usually has to collect everyone's food order, which often leads to confusion, missed orders, or incorrect meal counts.Easy Meal makes this process easier by allowing everyone to quickly select their meal preferences and place their orders in one place. The system keeps track of the orders so that meals can be managed properly and calculations at the end of the month become simple and clear.This project was created as a small solution to make hostel life a little more organized and convenient for everyone.
